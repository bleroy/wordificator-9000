Wordificator-9000

A helper for word games...

Enter the pattern for the word as a list of: number of letters, a colon, and the list of possible letters. For example, "2:abc 3:fiir" means a 5 letter word starting with 2 letters that can be a, b, or c, then 3 letters that can be f, i or r, and there may be two i's.
